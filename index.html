About Us

Platform Fokus is a community site that enables residents to share their damage procedure experiences with the members. Fokus also allows the members to upload and store pictures of their documents and damages they have in their houses. The platform allows communication between the members either privately through chat and also group posting for all members. The people have a right to decide who can view the information they share, creating privacy.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Platform Fokus?

    Platform Fokus is an existing platform on which residents of Steendam and Tjuchem can share images and documents related to damage due to gas extraction. They can share this with eachother and several organisations to improve communication and build a stronger case.

  • Why this platform?

    The basis of this idea is the existing Platform Fokus, which helps residents of the earthquake-area in Groningen communicate with each other. The added features that are proposed are based on the lack of clarity and speed in the current damage assessment process. We see the opportunity of combining this process on one communication platform. Also access to data by all the organisations involved will make sharing more accessible.

  • How did this website come to existence?

    This website is part of an intervention proposal by students of the ITC faculty at the University of Twente. It is part of a course surrounding human-induced earthquakes, with the Province of Groningen as case-study area.
